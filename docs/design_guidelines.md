# TITAN Design System: Global Infrastructure Authority

## 1. Aesthetic Vision

The TITAN design system is built to convey **Institutional Authority**, **Security**, and **Sovereign Scale**. It moves away from "playful" UI towards a "Serious International Corporation" aesthetic (think high-frequency trading, global infrastructure, or state-level intelligence).

- **Core Principles:** Architectural lines, high contrast, zero border-radius, and asymmetric structural precision.
- **Tone:** Objective, authoritative, and systemic.

---

## 2. Color System

TITAN uses two high-contrast modes.

### TITAN DARK (Primary/Default)

- **Background Deep:** `#212121`
- **Background Secondary:** `#171717`
- **Text Header:** `#ffffff` (Pure White)
- **Text Body:** `#d1d1d1` (Muted Grey)
- **Accent:** `#007aff` (Infrastructure Blue)
- **Structural Lines:** `#3d3d3d` (Subtle separators)

### TITAN LIGHT (Institutional Clarity)

- **Background Primary:** `#ffffff`
- **Background Secondary:** `#f5f5f7`
- **Text Header:** `#000000`
- **Text Body:** `#333333`
- **Accent:** `#0055ff`

---

## 3. Typography

**Primary Font:** `Inter` (or strictly utilitarian sans-serif).

- **Headers:** All caps, wide tracking (letter-spacing: 0.05em).
- **Body:** Technocratic, highly legible.
- **Data:** Monospace numbers for tabular data.

---

## 4. UI Components

### Containers & Cards

- **Border Radius:** `0px` (Strictly square).
- **Borders:** 1px solid structural lines (`#1a1a1a` in dark mode).
- **Shadows:** None. Depth is created via contrast and distinct borders.

### Interactive Elements (Buttons/Inputs)

- **Buttons:**
  - Rectangular.
  - Ghost outlines or solid high-contrast blocks.
  - Uppercase labels.
  - Hover: Sharp color inversion or "glitch" underline.
- **Inputs:**
  - No background (transparent).
  - Bottom border only or full hairline box.
  - Placeholder text in uppercase.

---

## 5. Layout & Grid

- **Density:** High. Designed for information density.
- **Spacing:** Mathematical (multiples of 4px or 8px).
- **Separators:** Visible grid lines are encouraged to denote "structure".
